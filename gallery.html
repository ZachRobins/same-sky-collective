How buying works

1. Find a piece

Browse here or visit the studio — the full collection is always on the walls at 417 Tranquille Rd.

2. Inquire

Tap "Inquire to buy" and we'll confirm availability, payment, and pickup or local delivery.

3. The artist gets paid

70% goes directly to the artist. The remaining 30% keeps the lights on and the doors open.
