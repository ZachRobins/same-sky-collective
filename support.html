Support the Collective

Artists keep 70% of every sale — which means we run on community. Here's how to help keep the lights on.

Donate now
Give

Donate

Your donation pays rent, utilities, and art supplies — and funds paid peer groups for people who are usually asked to give their time for free.

What your gift does:

$25 — art supplies for one artist for a month
$100 — pays peers for a community group session
$500 — keeps the studio open for a week

Ways to give

Wire these up to the society's real accounts — e.g. CanadaHelps (handles tax receipts for registered charities), PayPal Giving, e-transfer, or Stripe Payment Link. One-click, under two minutes, mobile-first.
